If you are on Drupal 9, use Upgrade Status on your Drupal 9 site to get an estimate of the work needed. The plan provided by the module will tell you where to use drupal-rector to update your own custom code and where to help contributed projects update their code.

Drupal 8 reached its end of life in November 2021! You must update to Drupal 9 first to update to Drupal 10 later on. Use Upgrade Status to analyze your site. With over 9,000 D9 ready projects, and more than 2,500 projects ready for D10 even before its release, there's a good chance everything you need is ready.

Drupal 7 will be end of life in January 2025. Yes, this is after the planned release of Drupal 10. While Drupal 10 will include migration tools from Drupal 7, it is much more likely that the contributed projects you use are available for Drupal 9 already. Don't wait! Start updating to Drupal 9 soon. The move to Drupal 10 will be way easier from Drupal 9.

Commitment to accessibility—taking out-of-the-box accessibility to a new level, as we prioritize accessibility with each newly added feature and interface.

Content hub for web, mobile, and more— its structured content model and flexible web service APIs serve all your channels from one source.

Powerful editorial digital management tools—with reusable media and publication workflows, your marketing team has the latest tools at their fingertips.

API-first architecture—At the forefront of headless/decoupled architecture, Drupal enables you to integrate with next-generation channels and devices.
